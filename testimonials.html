From Students

Exams sat, scores earned.

“

I was going to skip my Calculus exam because I couldn't cover the fee in time. Prosperity Pathways funded it before the registration deadline, and I ended up scoring a 4 — enough for college credit I didn't think I'd get.

JD
Jordan D.
AP Calculus AB, Class of 2025

“

My counselor mentioned the foundation two weeks before the deadline. There was no essay, no long application — just a quick referral, and three of my exam fees were covered. I passed all three.

MR
Maria R.
AP Biology & AP Spanish, Class of 2024

“

I qualified for a fee waiver but still couldn't afford the prep materials. The practice sets Prosperity Pathways sent over were put together by students who'd actually taken the exam, which made a real difference on test day.

TK
Tyler K.
AP U.S. History, Class of 2025
From Schools

No student misses an exam over cost.

“

As an AP coordinator, the registration deadline is always the hardest part of the year for our lowest-income students. Prosperity Pathways confirmed funding for nine students within days of our referral, well ahead of the cutoff.

PC
Priya C.
AP Coordinator, Placeholder High School

“

We partnered with the foundation last year and it changed how many of our students even attempt AP exams. Knowing the fee isn't a barrier, counselors can focus on whether a student is ready — not whether they can pay.

DW
David W.
School Counselor, Placeholder School District
