Our Mission

Opportunity starts here.

We fully fund AP exam fees for high school students with financial need.

Donate now Get involved

Every year, thousands of academically prepared students are forced to forgo AP exams—not because they lack the ability to succeed, but because the cost places the opportunity out of reach. For many families, the expense of multiple AP exams can create an impossible financial burden, preventing students from earning the college credit and recognition they have worked hard to achieve.

Prosperity Pathways AP Foundation exists to remove that barrier. By fully funding AP exam fees for students with demonstrated financial need, we make sure a single fee never stands between a student and the future they've already earned.

We work directly through schools rather than around them. We identify students who may qualify and confirm their eligibility together with the school, then fund the exam fee directly and register it before the deadline.

The road ahead is straightforward: deepen our partnerships, expand to new schools, provide preparation resources, and ensure no eligible student in our partner districts misses an exam over cost.

Growth, for us, is measured in registrations confirmed and scores reported.
