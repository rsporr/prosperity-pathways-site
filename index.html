Our Mission

Talent, not tuition.

We fully fund AP exam fees and provide free preparation for high school students with financial need.

Donate now Get involved

Every spring, capable students decide not to sit for AP exams they have prepared for all year — not because they aren't ready, but because the fee is out of reach. A single exam costs nearly a hundred dollars. For a student taking three, that is a month of a parent's groceries.

Prosperity Pathways AP Foundation exists to remove that barrier. We fully fund AP exam fees and provide free preparation resources to high school students with financial need, so that talent — not tuition — determines who gets ahead.

We work directly through schools rather than around them. Counselors know which students qualify and which exams they intend to take; we fund those fees at the source and confirm registration before the deadline.

Alongside funding, every supported student receives free, curated preparation materials — practice sets, review guides, and study plans — assembled by recent AP students who sat the same exams.

In our first year we funded exams across two partner schools. The road ahead is straightforward: deepen those partnerships, formalize our prep library, and reach every eligible student in our partner districts by 2027.

Growth, for us, is measured in registrations confirmed and scores reported — never in overhead.
