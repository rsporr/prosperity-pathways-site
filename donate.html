Where your gift goes

Every gift is applied directly to exam fees and preparation materials. Because we are student-run, nearly every dollar reaches a student. Choose an amount below, or give any sum that suits you.

Here's what your gift funds:

  • $25 — helps cover part of an exam fee
  • $50 — half of one AP exam registration fee
  • $99 — one full AP exam fee for a prepared student Recommended
  • $198 — two students' exam fees, fully covered
  • $500+ — a full cohort through exam day
