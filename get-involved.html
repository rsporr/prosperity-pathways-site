Get Involved

Four ways to open the door

Whether you teach, study, give, or volunteer, there is a place for you here.

Home›Get Involved
For Students

If the fee is your only barrier, ask.

If you qualify for free or reduced-price lunch, a fee waiver, or simply can't cover AP exam costs, talk to your counselor and have them refer you to us. There is no lengthy application and no essay — we confirm eligibility through your school and fund your registration directly.

Talk to your counselor
For Schools

Partner with us in one conversation.

Counselors and AP coordinators can refer eligible students through a short, secure process. We handle funding at the source and confirm registration before deadlines, so no prepared student in your building misses an exam over cost.

Become a partner school
For Volunteers

Build the prep library.

Recent AP students and educators help assemble and review our free preparation materials — practice sets, review guides, and study plans. It is flexible, remote, and meaningful: the resources you make reach students sitting the same exams you did.

Volunteer with us
For Donors

Fund exams, not overhead.

Gifts go directly to exam fees and prep materials. Because we are student-run, nearly every dollar reaches a student. A single gift of $97 covers one full AP exam for a student who has already done the work.
